Purpose-built for Tonga's builders, contractors and tradespeople — trade pricing, credit terms and dedicated service at every PTH store.

Trade Customers
Built for the Trade

Your Account, Your Terms

A PTH Trade Account is designed for working professionals — builders, contractors, electricians, plumbers and tradespeople who rely on a consistent, well-priced supply of materials to run their businesses and complete their projects on time.

With a Trade Account you get trade-only pricing, 30-day credit terms, priority counter service and a single monthly statement that makes it easy to manage your costs. All three PTH stores honour your account — one card, the whole Kingdom.

What Your Trade Account Includes

Trade Pricing

Exclusive trade-only pricing across the full PTH product range — building materials, timber, hardware, tools and more.

30-Day Credit Terms

Buy what you need now, pay at end of month. Simple monthly statements, no per-transaction charges.

Priority Counter Service

Skip the queue. Trade account holders get priority service at the trade counter at all three PTH stores.

Valid at All Three Stores

One trade account accepted at Nukuʻalofa, Ha'apai and Vava'u — no need for separate accounts on each island.

Multiple Card Holders

Add your foremen and site managers to your account. Set individual spending limits for each cardholder.

Purchase History

Itemised monthly statements showing all purchases by date, store and cardholder — great for job costing.

Eligibility

Who Can Open a Trade Account?

PTH Trade Accounts are available to any registered business or sole trader operating in the Kingdom of Tonga who purchases building materials, hardware or related products on a regular basis.

  • Builders & construction contractors
  • Electricians & plumbers
  • Carpenters & joiners
  • Property developers & landlords
  • Hardware resellers & merchants
  • Government & NGO procurement

How to Open Your Trade Account

01

Visit Any PTH Store

Come in to any of our three stores and speak with the trade desk. Bring your business registration documents and a form of ID.

02

Complete the Application

Fill in a short application form with your business details, expected monthly spend and preferred credit limit.

03

Credit Assessment

Our accounts team reviews your application. Most accounts are approved within 2 business days.

04

Start Trading

Receive your trade card, set up additional cardholders and start purchasing on trade terms straight away.
