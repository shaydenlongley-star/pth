Our Construction Team

Quality Builds, Every Time

PTH's construction division brings together decades of experience building in Tonga's unique environment — tropical heat, high humidity, cyclone exposure and seismic risk. We understand what it takes to build structures that stand up to the Kingdom's conditions and last for generations.

Because we supply our own materials, we can guarantee product quality, control costs and keep projects moving without the supply delays that slow down other contractors. One team, one account, start to finish.

The PTH Advantage

  • No material mark-up from a third-party supplier
  • Guaranteed product quality — we know exactly what goes in
  • Cyclone and seismic-rated materials sourced and specified correctly
  • No supply delays stopping your project mid-build
  • Single point of accountability for your entire build
