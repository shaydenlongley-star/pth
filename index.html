Who We Are

Your Partner in Building Tonga

Pacific Timber & Hardware has been at the heart of Tonga's construction and agricultural sector for decades. We supply everything from individual tradespeople and farmers through to large commercial contractors and government projects across the Kingdom.

With stores in Nukuʻalofa, Ha'apai and Vava'u, we understand the local challenges — cyclone and earthquake resilience, island logistics, sourcing quality products — and we're here to make your job easier.
