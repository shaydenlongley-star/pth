Northern Tonga
Serving the North

Vava'u's Hardware Specialist

The Vava'u store is PTH's northern branch, serving Neiafu and the wider Vava'u island group. Known for its stunning natural harbour and growing tourism sector, Vava'u also has a strong agricultural community and active construction market — and our store is fully stocked to support it.

Whether you're a local builder, a farmer or a homeowner carrying out renovations, the Vava'u store carries the same full product range, the same trade account and VIP programs as our Nukuʻalofa flagship.
