SDEP Program
About SDEP

More Than Just a Supplier

The PTH Sales Development & Expansion Program (SDEP) is designed for businesses across the Kingdom of Tonga that want to grow their hardware and building materials operation. Whether you're a small island retailer or an established merchant, SDEP gives you the backing of Tonga's largest hardware supplier.

We provide competitive wholesale pricing, dedicated account support, reliable supply and the training your team needs to sell with confidence. Your success is our success.

Program Benefits

What SDEP Gives You

Wholesale Pricing

Access PTH's wholesale price list across all product categories — building materials, hardware, tools and farm supplies.

Dedicated Account Manager

A named PTH account manager who knows your business, handles your orders and resolves issues fast.

Reliable Supply

Priority access to stock, regular container shipments and advance notification of supply changes so your shelves stay full.

Sales Support & Training

Product training, merchandising guides and marketing materials to help your team sell more effectively.

Island Delivery

Regular delivery schedules to Ha'apai and Vava'u, with freight arrangements for outer islands on request.

Brand & Territory Support

Territory arrangements available for qualifying dealers in outer island communities with limited competition.

Getting Started

How to Join SDEP

01

Initial Enquiry

Contact our sales team at any PTH store or by phone or email. Tell us about your business and what you need from a supplier.

02

Business Assessment

We assess your location, product needs and business volume to confirm you're a good fit for the SDEP program.

03

Agreement & Onboarding

We agree terms, set up your account, set your credit facility (if applicable) and introduce you to your account manager.

04

First Order & Ongoing Support

Place your first order and start trading. Your account manager checks in regularly to ensure everything is running smoothly.
