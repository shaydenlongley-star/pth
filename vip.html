PTH VIP Rewards

VIP Members
About VIP Rewards

Shop More, Save More

The PTH VIP Rewards program is our way of saying thank you to our loyal customers across the Kingdom of Tonga. Every purchase earns you points — whether you're buying a bag of cement, a DeWalt power tool or a full container of roofing iron. Those points add up to real savings on future purchases.

Membership is free, sign-up takes two minutes at any store, and your card works at all three PTH locations — Nukuʻalofa, Ha'apai and Vava'u.

Member Benefits

Your VIP Privileges

Points on Every Purchase

Earn VIP points on every dollar spent at any PTH store. Points accumulate across all three locations with a single card.

Member-Only Discounts

Exclusive discounts on selected products, available only to VIP card holders. New offers added every month.

Early Sale Access

VIP members get advance notice of sales and seasonal promotions — and first access to clearance stock before it sells out.

Birthday Reward

A special bonus point credit to your account in your birthday month — our gift to you each year.

Valid Across All Stores

Your VIP card works at Nukuʻalofa, Ha'apai and Vava'u. One membership, the whole Kingdom.

Free to Join

There are no joining fees, no annual fees and no minimum spend. All PTH customers are welcome to join.

Membership Tiers

The More You Shop, The More You Earn

As your annual spend grows, you move to a higher tier — earning points faster and unlocking better benefits.

MEMBER

Standard

Starting tier for all VIP members. Earn 1 point per T$1 spent. Access member discounts and sale previews.

SILVER

Silver Member

Earn 1.5 points per T$1. Includes all Standard benefits plus enhanced birthday reward and priority counter service.

GOLD

Gold Member

Earn 2 points per T$1. Includes all Silver benefits plus exclusive Gold-only offers and dedicated account support.
