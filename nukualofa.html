Nukuʻalofa
Our Flagship Store

Tonga's Hardware Home Base

The Nukuʻalofa store is PTH's main branch and the largest hardware operation in the Kingdom. As the country's capital and commercial hub, Nukuʻalofa is where we stock our deepest range — every product category fully loaded and ready for builders, contractors and homeowners across Tongatapu.

Our full trade desk, VIP counter and construction team are all based here. If you're a first-time visitor or a long-time customer, our team is here to help you find exactly what you need.
