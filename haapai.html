Ha'apai Group
Serving the Central Islands

Ha'apai's Hardware Specialist

The Ha'apai store is PTH's central branch, serving Pangai and the wider Ha'apai island group. Ha'apai is one of Tonga's most exposed regions — and our store is stocked with the materials needed to build and rebuild resilient structures for the communities that call these islands home.

The same product range, the same trade account and VIP programs, and the same commitment to service as our Nukuʻalofa flagship — right here in Ha'apai.
